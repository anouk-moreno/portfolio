About Me

Hi, my name is Anouk. I studied Computer Science with a specialization in Statistics. Over the course of my studies, I was exposed to a wide range of topics related to software development, system behavior, and basic security concepts.

I would not describe myself as an expert in cybersecurity yet, but it is an area that has increasingly caught my attention, especially in relation to user privacy and real-world systems. At the same time, I am also interested in the financial domain and the challenges that arise when technology, data, and economics intersect.

During my degree, I worked on applied projects that allowed me to explore these topics, including my Bachelor’s thesis, which focused on mobile permission analysis and user awareness through a longitudinal study. This project helped me understand how technical decisions, data collection, and user behavior interact in practice.

I am still in the process of defining my long-term professional path, but for now I am focusing on projects that combine cybersecurity with economically relevant systems. Further technical details about my work can be found in the other sections of this portfolio.

On a more personal note, I plan to add access to my blog in the future, where I share reflections and interests beyond purely technical topics.
